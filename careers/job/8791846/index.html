Customer Sales Associate

Santa Ana, CA

Apply
Description

Position Summary

Responsible for initial customer contact, providing best-in-class customer service, and selling incremental products and options to customers at the rental desk. Additionally, they hold responsibility for processing rentals, resolving overdue rentals, responding to customer questions and complaints, and communicating with shuttle drivers for pick-ups.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • As a brand ambassador, ensures walk-up and reservation driven customers have a best-in-class car rental experience at the rental desk
  • Greets customers, completing the necessary rental and location information, while disclosing rates, terms and conditions of rental, obtaining customer initials and signatures on contract
  • Sells customers incremental sales products (e.g. coverage, toll passes, pre-paid fuel, etc.), explaining the value and cost, enabling the customer to make an informed decision
  • Maintains and updates rental agreement files, inclusive of contacting and notifying customers of overdue rental vehicles, inquiring on the expected date of return and processing extensions accordingly
  • Meets sales goals set for each location by selling vehicle upgrades and incremental sales products, tailored to the needs of each customer
  • Liaises with kiosk and fleet parking lot staff to identify vehicle availability to inform customers of vehicle options
  • Provide general assistance in person or over the phone to customers regarding where to pick up their vehicle, shuttle locations and processes (where applicable), as well as overall directions to their destination if and when required
  • Periodically may be required to move vehicles, or assist in other operational functions (e.g. supporting Kiosk operations)
  • Other duties as required

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or GED required
  • Minimum 1 year customer service or sales experience
  • Experience meeting sales quotas or working in highly commissioned positions preferred
  • Exceptional interpersonal and relationship building skills required
  • Ability to follow incentivized sales program
  • Demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong organizational skills and the ability to multi-task
  • Must have the ability to manage stressful or unusual situations in order to maintain good customer service
  • Must have basic computer navigation skills and knowledge of Microsoft Office Applications
  • Must be willing to wear company uniforms
  • Must be at least 21 years old and be able to pass pre-employment background and drug screens.
  • Must have a valid driver's license with a satisfactory driving record
  • Must have the flexibility to work day and/or evening hours, as well as weekends/holidays as needed
  • Must be authorized to work in the United States
  • Ability to communicate in other languages (Spanish, French, Portuguese, Hebrew, German, etc.) is a plus.
